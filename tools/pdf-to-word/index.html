⚠️ Conversion limitations
✓ Works best with PDFs containing selectable text.
  • Scanned documents and handwriting are not recognised because OCR is not included.
  • Images, charts, backgrounds, headers and footers are not transferred.
  • Complex tables, columns and page layouts may lose their original formatting.
  • Password-protected or damaged PDF files may not open.
  • The generated Word document prioritises editable text over an exact visual copy.
